Arduino/Genuino 101 (BLE)

The Arduino 101* (branded Genuino 101* outside the U.S.) board includes on-board Bluetooth LE to enable developers to interact with Bluetooth enabled devices such as phones and tablets.

See Arduino/Genuino 101 for information how to use the BLE module on the Arduino 101 with Zephyr.
