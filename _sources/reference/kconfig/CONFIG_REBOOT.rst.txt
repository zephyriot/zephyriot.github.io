.. title:: REBOOT

.. option:: CONFIG_REBOOT:
.. _CONFIG_REBOOT:

Enable the sys_reboot() API. Enabling this can drag in other subsystems
needed to perform a "safe" reboot (e.g. SYSTEM_CLOCK_DISABLE, to stop the
system clock before issuing a reset).
