.. title:: SPI_INTEL

.. option:: CONFIG_SPI_INTEL:
.. _CONFIG_SPI_INTEL:

Enable support for Intel's SPI controllers. Such controller
was formelly found on XScale chips. It can be found nowadays
on CEXXXX Intel media controller and Quark CPU (2 of them).
