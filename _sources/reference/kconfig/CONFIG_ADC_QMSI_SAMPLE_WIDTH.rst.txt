.. _CONFIG_ADC_QMSI_SAMPLE_WIDTH:

Defines ADC device data sample width (resolution):

- 5 = 6 bits resolution

- 7 = 8 bits resolution

- 9 = 10 bits resolution

- 11 = 12 bits resolution
