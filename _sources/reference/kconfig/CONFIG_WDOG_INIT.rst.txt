.. title:: WDOG_INIT

.. option:: CONFIG_WDOG_INIT:
.. _CONFIG_WDOG_INIT:

This processor enables the watchdog timer with a short timeout
upon reset. Therefore, this requires that the watchdog be configured
during reset handling.
