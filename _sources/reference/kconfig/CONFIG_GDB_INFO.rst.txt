.. title:: GDB_INFO

.. option:: CONFIG_GDB_INFO:
.. _CONFIG_GDB_INFO:

This option enables the kernel to collect additional information
during interrupts, exceptions, and context switches. This information
is required for task-aware debugging with GDB.

:Type:             bool
