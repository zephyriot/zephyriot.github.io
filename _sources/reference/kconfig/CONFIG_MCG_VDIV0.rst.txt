.. title:: MCG_VDIV0

.. option:: CONFIG_MCG_VDIV0:
.. _CONFIG_MCG_VDIV0:

Selects the amount to divide the VCO output of the PLL. The VDIV 0 bits
establish the multiplication factor (M) applied to the reference clock
frequency.
