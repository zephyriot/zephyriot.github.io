.. option:: CONFIG_LIS3MDL_ODR:
.. _CONFIG_LIS3MDL_ODR:

Magnetometer output data rate expressed in samples per second.
Data rates supported by the chip are 0.625, 1.25, 2.5, 5, 10, 20,
40, 80, 155, 300, 560 and 1000.
