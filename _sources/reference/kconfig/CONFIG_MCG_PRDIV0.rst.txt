.. title:: MCG_PRDIV0

.. option:: CONFIG_MCG_PRDIV0:
.. _CONFIG_MCG_PRDIV0:

Selects the amount to divide down the external reference clock for the PLL.
The resulting frequency must be in the range of 2 MHz to 4 MHz.
