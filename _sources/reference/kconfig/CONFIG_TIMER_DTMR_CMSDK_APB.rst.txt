.. title:: TIMER_DTMR_CMSDK_APB

.. option:: CONFIG_TIMER_DTMR_CMSDK_APB:
.. _CONFIG_TIMER_DTMR_CMSDK_APB:

The dualtimer (DTMR) present in the platform is used as a timer.
This option enables the support for the timer.
